What is Floating?
Floating is a unique environment created by the floatation tank. The tank and room are darkened, disconnecting the body from its sensory inputs allowing the brain to relax. The environment created allows our body to simply relax and take care of itself. This restricted environmental relaxation has been proven to enhance how the brain works. Thus, increasing theta brain waves which are activated by meditation and active during REM sleep. Floating provides you with a tranquility and relaxation that helps with the overwhelming stress of everyday life.
How does it work?
The tank is filled with water and about 1,000 pounds of dissolved Epsom salts, which suspends the body afloat without needing to swim or tread.
